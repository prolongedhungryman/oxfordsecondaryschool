A Mission Defined by Excellence

At Oxford Secondary School, our mission of discovery and learning is energized by a spirit of ambition and possibility rooted in Butwal since 1991. Here you'll find learner-centered teaching, fully digitalized classrooms, and a child-friendly environment that nurtures holistic development — physical, emotional, and intellectual.

Academic Programs

From Montessori to Higher Secondary — a complete educational journey under one institution.

Montessori / Pre-School
Play-based, child-led early learning that builds curiosity and confidence from the start.
Grade 1–5 Elementary
Core literacy, numeracy and life skills in a nurturing, activity-rich classroom environment.
Grade 6–8 Lower Secondary
Deeper subject exploration with critical thinking, labs, and co-curricular development.
Grade 9–10 SEE
Rigorous preparation for the national SEE examinations and future academic pathways.
Grade 11–12 Higher Secondary
Four specialised streams to match every aspiration.
